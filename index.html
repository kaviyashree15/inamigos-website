Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion and Action

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Central Government-licensed, Section 8 non-profit headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We believe every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and care — and we work tirelessly to make that a reality across India.

Our work spans food distribution, quality education, women's skill development, animal protection, and environmental sustainability — creating ripples of positive change in underserved communities.

✅ Section 8 Registered ✅ 80G and 12A Certified ✅ CSR-1 Registered ✅ NITI Aayog Listed ✅ ISO 9001:2015
Our Works

Flagship Projects & Initiatives

Six core programs addressing the most pressing needs of society — from hunger and illiteracy to environmental degradation and animal welfare.

Project SEVA

Providing nutritious food, clothing, and essential supplies to homeless individuals, daily-wage workers, and the underprivileged — because no one should sleep hungry.

Food & Clothing Relief
Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children by setting up learning centres, distributing stationery, and connecting young minds to brighter futures.

Education for All
Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill-development workshops, entrepreneurship training, and financial literacy programs — enabling independence and self-reliance.

Women Empowerment
Project Prakriti

Driving environmental conservation through tree-plantation drives, waste-management campaigns, and sustainability awareness — healing our planet, one step at a time.

Environmental Conservation
Project Prani

Protecting stray and injured animals through rescue operations, veterinary care, and adoption drives — because every life deserves compassion.

Animal Welfare
Project Akshara

Fighting adult illiteracy by running evening literacy classes and skill workshops for daily-wage workers and rural communities across India.

Adult Literacy
